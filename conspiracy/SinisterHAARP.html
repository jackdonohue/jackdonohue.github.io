The Facts

HAARP was created back in 1997, and $30 million has been invested into the project since it started.

The Theory

The theory behind this research, is that it could be more threatening than a nuclear bomb. People believe that this could be done by filling the sky with large amounts of energy rays from the antennas down in Alaska. These rays of energy could then potentially be reflected back into the Earth's ionosphere on an extremely low frequency, called ELF waves, which will cause mass destruction. Large amount of ELF waves cause vibrations, not just any vibrations though, very intense vibrations. If these ELF waves are reflected into Earths ionosphere they will enter your brain, and it will seriously mess you up. It could either make you go literally insane, or it could completely paralyze you.

The Reality

Nobody on Earch knows how these waves will react to our ionosphere. There is about 1 in 50 million of a chance that anything at all will happen when these waves are shot up in the sky. With our ozone layer, the ionosphere protects us all from burning to death by the sun, but HAARP could supposedly destroy it in seconds.
